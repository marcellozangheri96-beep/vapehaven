SWIX Mate 9000 Review: Is It Australia's Best Disposable Vape?

Published 10 March 2026 • Updated 26 March 2026 • 4 min read

The SWIX Mate 9000 has become one of the most talked-about disposable vapes in Australia. With nearly 9000 puffs per device, mesh coil technology, and a smart LED display showing battery and e-liquid levels, it promises a premium experience at an accessible price. After testing the full range of 19 flavors, we can confirm: the hype is justified. Here's our detailed review.

E-Liquid Capacity22ml
Battery2550mAh (Non-rechargeable)
CoilMesh Coil
DisplayLED (Battery + E-Liquid Level)
Dimensions103.5 × 40 × 26mm

Build Quality and Design

From the moment you pick up a SWIX Mate 9000, you can feel the quality. The device measures 103.5 × 40 × 26mm — compact and pocketable without feeling cheap. The plastic construction is solid, with no creaking or flex when you hold it firmly. This is important: many cheaper disposable vapes feel flimsy, but the SWIX Mate feels like it's designed to last.

The most distinctive feature is the integrated LED display on the front face. It's small but incredibly useful — it shows both your battery level and remaining e-liquid amount at a glance. No more wondering if you're about to hit a dead battery. In practice, this feature alone justifies the device's mid-range price. You always know exactly where you stand.

The mouthpiece is smooth and well-positioned, and the overall ergonomics feel thoughtful. Whether you're left or right-handed, the device sits naturally in your palm. For a single-use product, the attention to detail is genuinely impressive.

Performance and Flavor

The real test of any disposable vape is how it performs across its full lifespan. The SWIX Mate 9000 is powered by a mesh coil — and it shows. From the very first puff to the final draw, you get consistent, full-bodied flavor. Mesh coils heat e-liquid more evenly than traditional wire coils, which means richer taste and smoother vapor production throughout the device's life.

We tested every flavor in the lineup, and the mesh coil shines across all of them. Whether you're vaping fruity blends like Strawberry Watermelon or mint variants like Miami Mint, the flavor profile remains vibrant and accurate from start to finish. There's no noticeable degradation as you approach the end of the device's life — a common complaint with cheaper alternatives.

Battery performance is equally impressive. The 2550mAh battery is engineered to last exactly as long as the 22ml e-liquid supply. You won't finish your liquid with a dead battery, and you won't finish your battery with liquid left over. That's rare in the disposable vape market and shows genuine engineering precision.

Vapor production is moderate to heavy, striking a good balance for all-day use without being so dense that it feels overwhelming. The temperature of the vapor is warm without being hot, suggesting good coil efficiency.

Value for Money

At $45 AUD per device (down from the original $69), the SWIX Mate 9000 delivers genuinely strong value. That works out to approximately 0.5 cents per puff — competitive with other premium 9000-puff devices on the Australian market, but with superior flavor delivery thanks to the mesh coil.

When purchased in bundle packs, the value improves dramatically. A bundle of five devices drops the per-unit cost significantly, and the Mystery Box (10 random flavors) offers savings of up to 45% compared to single purchases. For regular users or those wanting to explore flavors, bundles are the smart choice.

It's also worth noting that Vaperoo includes free shipping across Australia, which many competitors don't offer at this price point. That alone can save you $8-15 per order on shipping costs.

The Verdict

The SWIX Mate 9000 delivers on every promise. The mesh coil produces noticeably richer flavor than cheaper competitors. The battery comfortably outlasts the e-liquid supply without wasting capacity. The LED display is genuinely useful rather than a gimmick. The build quality feels premium for the price.

Is it Australia's best disposable vape? In terms of raw performance, consistency, and value, it's absolutely in the conversation. At $45 per device with free shipping, it represents excellent value for anyone seeking a reliable, long-lasting disposable vape with premium flavor delivery.

If you vape daily and care about flavor quality, the SWIX Mate 9000 should be your go-to choice. If you're budget-conscious and want to sample multiple flavors, bundle packs offer unbeatable savings. Either way, the SWIX Mate 9000 is a standout in a crowded market.
